Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about HarmonyAI receptionists.

How does the AI receptionist handle complex customer inquiries?

Our AI is trained on thousands of real customer service interactions and can handle a wide variety of inquiries. For particularly complex issues, it can seamlessly transfer the call to a human operator or schedule a callback.

Can I customize the AI's voice and personality?

Yes! We offer multiple voice options and personality traits to match your brand identity. You can adjust the tone, pace, and even specific phrases to ensure the AI represents your business appropriately.

What kind of businesses benefit most from HarmonyAI?

While any business can benefit, we've seen particular success with medical practices, law firms, salons, real estate agencies, and small to medium-sized businesses that receive frequent calls for appointments and basic information.

How quickly can I set up HarmonyAI for my business?

Most businesses can be up and running within 24 hours. Our onboarding process is simple and we provide dedicated support to ensure a smooth transition.
